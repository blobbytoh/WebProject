About NYP Golf Club

Discover our rich history, passionate team, and commitment to excellence in their golf education

Our History

Established in 1992, NYP Golf Club has been a cornerstone of golf excellence at Nanyang Polytechnic for over three decades. What started as a small group of passionate golf enthusiasts has grown into one of Singapore's most prestigious polytechnic golf clubs, producing numerous professional golfers and champions.

Our club has evolved from humble beginnings to become a training ground for future golf professionals, with alumni now competing in international tournaments and working in the golf industry worldwide.

Our Vision

To be the leading polytechnic golf club in Singapore, nurturing future golf professionals and promoting the sport among young enthusiasts while maintaining the highest standards of excellence and sportsmanship.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive golf education, professional training, and character development opportunities that prepare our members for success in golf and life, while fostering a supportive community of golf enthusiasts.
