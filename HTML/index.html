Why Join Us?

Bonding & Networking  

Bonding and networking are important aspects of personal and professional growth. Through meaningful interactions, people are able to develop strong relationships. Besides socialising in school, we also have plenty of events in the field. Sessions with professional golf players could also improve your skills and knowledge, these are rare opportunities.

Interest & Talents  

When we try new activities—like joining a CCA, learning a new skill, or exploring a hobby—we learn more about what we enjoy and what we’re good at. This helps us gain confidence, stay motivated, and make better choices for our future..Some people may have hidden talents that is yet discovered so come join, you could always be the next Jack Nicklaus (American Retired Professional Golfer)

Character & Discipline  

Character building and discipline are key to becoming responsible and respectful individuals. Through consistent effort, such as participating in CCAs or taking on responsibilities, we learn values like honesty, perseverance, and self-control. Discipline helps us stay focused on our goals, while strong character guides us to make the right choices.
